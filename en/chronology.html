Our Journey

Kennel Chronology

From our very first dogs to today — a story written in paw prints.

Our History

Oldest at the top, newest at the bottom

✦
2005

Opening of “Bernerpetrinia” Kennel

The very beginning of our breeding journey. Bernerpetrinia was founded with a love for large, noble breeds and a commitment to responsible breeding.

2005 –

First Bernese Mountain Dogs

Welcoming our first Bernese Mountain Dogs — majestic, gentle giants who set the foundation for our breeding philosophy.

Early years

First Irish Setter

Our first Irish Setter joined the family — an elegant, spirited breed that further shaped our appreciation for quality and temperament.

Pre-2021

First Cavalier King Charles Spaniels

Falling in love with the Cavalier — their soulful eyes, gentle nature, and joyful spirit made them an instant and permanent part of our life.

New chapter

Opening of Matej’s Angels Kennel

A new name, a new chapter. Matej’s Angels was established, dedicated exclusively to Cavalier King Charles Spaniels and — later — Pomeranians.

September 2021

First Cavalier Litter — Litter A

Seven beautiful puppies — Adam, Atila, Axel David, Astra, Aria, Anemona, and Azalea — marked the first litter under the Matej’s Angels name.

2023 –

Breed Assessment Grade 5 — Own Breeding

Dali, bred by Matej’s Angels from Litter D (March 2023), achieved a perfect Grade 5 in his official breed assessment — a proud milestone for our own-bred line.

2025

First Pomeranians Join the Family

Kimmy, our Sable Pomeranian girl, became the first of a new breed chapter at Matej’s Angels — bringing her bold personality and magnificent coat.

2026

First Pomeranian Litter

Our first Pomeranian litter — a new milestone and the opening of a brand-new chapter at Matej’s Angels. Stay tuned for updates!

Matej's Angels Kennel

A royal kennel for Cavalier King Charles Spaniels and Pomeranians, based in Croatia.
