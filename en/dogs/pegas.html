"Pegas"

A noble Tricolor Cavalier male from the prestigious Scarlett Team kennel in Slovakia, Pegas graced our lives from 2011 until 2024. He carried champion bloodlines from the United Kingdom, Czech Republic, France, and Hungary - most notably through his father, the multi-champion Chianti z Vrchu králů. Gentle and proud, Pegas left a lasting mark on our kennel and our hearts.
