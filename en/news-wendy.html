Wendy received top mark at breeding evaluation in Rovinj

Our Wendy, Verduras Malcriado de la Casa, received a top mark at the breeding evaluation show in Rovinj.

22 September 2025 · News · By Sanja Volner

Our Wendy, Verduras Malcriado de la Casa (thank you Marcela Hroncová and Michaela Hroncová) received a top mark of 5 at the breeding evaluation show yesterday in Rovinj - another excellent result, we are very proud, Matej's Angels Kennel!

Descriptive evaluation: Good bone, in good condition, head of good proportions, scissor bite, eyes dark almond-shaped, ears well set and carried, neck of medium length, straight longer back, correct angulation of legs, correct movement.
