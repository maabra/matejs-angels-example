Our Story

About Matej's Angels

A family kennel built on love, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to the finest breed standards.

Welcome

Our Kennel,
Our Passion

✦

Matej's Angels is a small, family-run kennel located in Croatia, dedicated to breeding Cavalier King Charles Spaniels and Pomeranians of the highest quality. Every dog in our care is raised as a member of our family; loved, nurtured, and socialized from the very first day.

Our goal is not quantity but quality; healthy, well-tempered, beautifully typey dogs that bring joy to their families and represent their breeds with pride in the show ring.

Meet Our Dogs

What We Stand For

The principles that guide everything we do

✦

Health First

All our breeding dogs undergo comprehensive health testing according to breed club requirements. We test for MVD, SM/CM, eyes, hips, and genetic markers relevant to each breed.

Raised With Love

Our puppies grow up in our home, fully socialized with children, other dogs, and everyday sounds. They are handled daily, exposed to enrichment, and ready for any loving home.

Show Excellence

We breed to the FCI standard and regularly exhibit at national and international dog shows. Our dogs have earned titles across Europe, proving that beauty and health go hand in hand.

Lifelong Support

When you adopt a puppy from us, we become your partner for life. We offer guidance on nutrition, grooming, training, and health throughout your dog's entire life.

Ethical Breeding

We carefully plan every litter, select only proven, health-tested parents, and never breed for quantity. Every puppy is placed in a carefully screened, loving home.

Community

We are proud members of the Croatian Cynological Federation (HKS) and our respective breed clubs, registered under FCI 103/21. We believe in sharing knowledge and contributing to the betterment of our breeds.

Our Breeds

Two extraordinary breeds, one shared standard of excellence

✦

Cavalier King Charles Spaniel

One of the most beloved toy breeds in the world, the Cavalier combines the grace of a show dog with the gentle, affectionate nature of a true companion. Known for their soulful eyes, silky coat, and unfaltering love for their family.

At Matej's Angels, our Cavaliers come in all four recognised colours: Blenheim, Tricolour, Black & Tan, and Ruby; each carrying the hallmark sweetness of the breed.

View Our Cavaliers

Pomeranian

A bold, vivacious spirit in a tiny, magnificent package. Pomeranians captivate with their lush double coat, fox-like expression, and larger-than-life personality. Intelligent, curious, and endlessly charming.

Our Pomeranians represent various colours and are bred for correct type, compact body, and the characteristic bright, alert expression the breed is celebrated for worldwide.
