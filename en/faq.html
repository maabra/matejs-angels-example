Puppies & Purchasing

Contact us via our contact form or email. We will have a conversation to understand your lifestyle, home, and expectations. Once we agree you are a good match, we will arrange a reservation. A deposit is required to secure your puppy.

Availability varies. We plan litters carefully and announce them on our website and social media. We encourage you to join our waiting list - this way we can match you with the perfect puppy from our next planned litter.

Our puppies leave with: FCI pedigree registration, veterinary health check, age-appropriate vaccinations, microchip, EU pet passport, deworming record, starter pack (food sample, blanket with familiar scent), and our lifelong breeder support.

We release our puppies no earlier than 8–10 weeks of age. For some smaller Pomeranians, we may keep them longer (up to 12 weeks) to ensure they are robust and fully socialised before leaving us.

Yes, we have placed puppies throughout Europe. International buyers must arrange appropriate travel documentation. We can assist with guidance, but the logistics and costs of transport are the buyer's responsibility.

Health & Care

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels: MVD (Mitral Valve Disease) grading by cardiologist, MRI screening for Syringomyelia/Chiari Malformation, BVA/KC Eye scheme, DNA tests for relevant genetic diseases.

Pomeranians: Cardiac evaluation, eye certification, patella evaluation, DNA tests. Full results are available on each dog's individual page.

We feed our dogs a premium, high-quality dry kibble supplemented with fresh foods. We will share our current feeding regimen with your puppy's food sample. We recommend consulting your vet when transitioning foods.

Cavaliers have a silky, medium-length coat that requires brushing 3–4 times per week and occasional trimming. Their ears need regular attention to prevent matting and infections.

Pomeranians have a profuse double coat that benefits from daily brushing. Professional grooming every 6–8 weeks is recommended to maintain their beautiful appearance.

About Our Kennel

Yes. Matej's Angels is a registered kennel with the Croatian Cynological Federation (HKS), which is affiliated with the FCI (Fédération Cynologique Internationale). All our litters are registered and puppies receive internationally recognised FCI pedigrees.

We welcome visits by appointment. We prefer to meet potential owners in person (or via video call for international buyers). This allows us to ensure the match is right for both the puppy and your family.

Some of our males are available at stud to approved bitches from health-tested, registered kennels. Please contact us with details of your bitch (pedigree, health tests, photos) for consideration.
