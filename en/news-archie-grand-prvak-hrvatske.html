Archie is Grand Champion of Croatia

Congratulations to owner Alina Te and sire Archie — proudly from the Matej's Angels team.

5 June 2022 · News · By Sanja Volner

Dear families of our A and B litters, we are proud to announce — alongside the owner — that the sire of your puppies, Lord of the Manor's Merlin aka Archie, has become Grand Champion of Croatia! Congratulations to owner Alina Te and to sire Archie, of course, from the proud team at Matej's Angels.

SHARED FROM:
Alina Te
Dear friends!
I cannot describe our happiness!!!
Archie (Lord of the Manor’s Merlin from the Visnja Madile kennel) closed his third championship today and thus became

🏆🏆🏆
